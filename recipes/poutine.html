Poutine

Description

This recipe is a very simple one but oh so delicious. A classic meal that everyone can enjoy!

Ingredients

  • Potatoes
  • Gravy
  • Chese curds
  • Cooking oil

Steps

  1. Wash the potatoes and cut them the size you desire, or approximatively 1/4 inch, skin on.
  2. Poor the cooking oil in a deep saucepan ort in a deep fryer.
  3. Heat the oil until it reaches 340F on a thermometer and add the cut potatoes.
  4. Cook the fries for 10 minutes, then take them out and gently pat them from the oil.
  5. Bring the oil to 370F and cook again for 5-10 minutes or until golden.
  6. Drain the fries and put aside.
  7. Poor your beef gravy in a sauce pan and bring to ebullition.
  8. Once the sauce is ready, put the fries in a plate, add the cheese curds on top and then the gravy.
  9. Add toppings if desired (meat, olives, mushrooms, onions, etc.)
  10. Enjoy!
